ABOUT US

J MOLL Designer: Wesley Jay Moloto

With the humble beginnings of a single store around Limpopo in polokwane J MOLL opened by Wesley Jay Moloto, J Moll is still under development and growth from strength-to-strength to become synonymous with the excellence of the exclusive international brands it offers in store. J Moll is a brand that was started around 2017

Each of the J Moll brands or stores aims to successfully deliver its brand all around the country, since it has been designed to be an inviting place for customers to not only have access to top quality exclusive International clothes brands, but also a memorable shopping experience that will keep them coming back for more.

With a brand built on great relationships, trustworthiness and outstanding quality, J Moll has continually managed to establish, and nurture, a loyalty with its customers that has been handed down from generation to generation.

J Moll empowers all those who wear its brands to exude confidence, no matter whether they’re at home or waltzing down the red carpet. Known for selling desirable, premium quality brands, J Moll is the aspirational clothes retailer of choice.


Now a highly-trusted brand in the industry, J Moll combines the finest raw materials and production methods with cutting edge design, to bring you high-quality, fashionable clothes, bags, watches and caps that won’t hurt your back.
